                              \ma &\Gamma, \Gamma', p; \Delta_N; \Xi_N; \Gamma_{N1}; \Delta_{N1}; \cdot; \Omega; [\Gamma'; \Delta_N; \cdot; \bang p; \cdot; \Omega; \cdot; q, \Upsilon], P_1, P; AG; \Omega_N; \Delta_N; T \rightarrow \Xi'; \Delta'; \Gamma'
                        \end{split}
}
\]
